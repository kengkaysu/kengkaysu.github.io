Kyla Castillo

Kyla Castillo also knows as “Kengkay” ,”kaye”, ”kengkaysu”, and commonly as “Kyy" was born on June 3rd, making her a gemini. She is a simple girl who lives a simple life and comes from a simple family. She was born in 2002 at Marikina City where she also grew up. She is the second child of the couple Victor R. Castillo Sr. and Yolanda A. Castillo while being the only female of five siblings, despite being the second oldest.

Kyla is a kind and courteous young woman who is also said to be excellent at speaking and socializing, as well as engaging with others. She also got this wise way of thinking, she opts to think carefully before acting and a very thoughtful person to avoid trouble that she really hates. She is also a quite reliable and very caring to her friends. In terms of work, she is lazy when it comes to house chores, but, she is studious and serious about her studies and interest. Despite the fact that Kyla is focused on her studies, she never forgets to socialize and have fun with her family and relatives, with whom she loves spending time no matter what day of the week it is.
